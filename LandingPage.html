Comic Mentor

So many social media sites are homes for artists. However, a platform not tailored to them leaves them lost and a bit confused at times. Comic Mentor's goal is to allow artists to interact, learn, and share with the intent of growing as artists. You can feel free to promote without being lost in a platform that promotes popular posts and gives little chance to grow attention. Comic Mentor also allows artists to do peer review and critiques.

Comic Mentor strives to be a place where creators can learn and thrive. One of the key features are allowing artists to work together to improve themselves. We have both a reviewed section of learning resources and a community section that allows for peer learning.
Our community features allow artists a place to come together to not only learn, but collaborate on projects as well. Whether it be a direct collaboration or a themed event.
Comic Mentor's goal is to help artists hone their skills and work towards being able to publish their work. Because of this goal there are spaces to advertise any published work along with any commissions postings.

Research

Generally when I talked to people, one of the main concerns with artists using social media was that it was extremely hard to get noticed. The way most sites work is that they suggest and promote already popular posts. That makes it insanely hard to start out as an artist.
